Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leaders

Discover how a small retail shop in Durgad Bail transformed into one of Karnataka's most trusted notebook manufacturers.

Company Profile

Founded in 2017 by Manjayya Bhavimath alongside his elder brother, Eskay Industries began as a small retail shop in Noolvi Road before relocating to Nekar Nagar, Hubli in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic. What started as a modest venture has grown into a thriving manufacturing hub serving customers across Karnataka.

Their mission remains unwavering: to expand their business while providing quality products to customers all over India. Before establishing Eskay Industries, the founders operated a retail shop in Durgad Bail, Hubli, which laid the foundation for their journey into the wholesale market.

Our Journey

The company began as Nataraj Book Traders, a small retail shop in Durgad Bail, Hubli. They purchased books from other companies and sold them in the market. Gradually, as they expanded their inventory to include notebooks, A4 sheets, card sheets, and other stationery items, they built customer trust and grew their market presence.

Armed with increasing popularity, they transitioned into the wholesale market, supplying to retail shops across Hubli and beyond. Their commitment to timely delivery opened doors to more customers and larger orders.

Today, the manufacturing facility spans 8,000-8,500 square feet in Nekar Nagar, producing a diverse range of notebooks and stationery products under the trusted brand name "SCHOOLMATE."
